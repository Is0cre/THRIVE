# THRIVES — Psychology Advisors

## Why Advisors Matter
- Builder has no formal degree — advisor names add clinical credibility
- App stores (especially Google Play health category) scrutinise health apps
- Grant applications strengthened by named professionals
- NGO clients more likely to trust clinically advised tool
- Does not affect builder's ownership or control

---

## What Advisors Are Asked to Do
- Review feature set for clinical soundness
- Advise on safety protocols and referral language
- Provide name and credentials for advisory credits screen
- Optional: contribute to documentation or grant applications
- NOT: take ownership, control direction, or receive equity

## Ideal Advisor Profile
- Trauma-informed practitioner (EMDR certified ideal)
- Somatic therapy background useful
- Open to FOSS / privacy-first health tools
- Not affiliated with big tech or pharma

---

## How to Approach Them
Draft message:

"Hey [name], I'm building an open source trauma regulation app — completely free, no data collection, privacy-first. It combines EMDR bilateral stimulation, binaural beats, HRV biofeedback and grounding tools. I'd love to have your name as a psychology advisor on the project if you're open to it — mainly for credibility and to make sure the clinical foundations are sound. No equity, no commitment beyond occasional feedback. Interested?"
